Why Dr. Allahbadia Chooses Robotics

  • check_circle Sub-millimeter accuracy in bone preparation — no CT scans or pre-op MRI required with the CORI system.
  • check_circle Real-time soft tissue balancing for a natural-feeling joint that lasts longer.
  • check_circle Significantly reduced post-operative pain — patients report faster discharge and less dependency on pain medication.
  • check_circle Intra-operative 3D modelling ensures every surgery is tailored to your specific anatomy, not a standard template.

Metric CORI Robotic Surgery Traditional Surgery
