About This Page

Why Complex Cases Matter

Not every knee problem is straightforward. Some patients arrive after failed surgeries elsewhere. Others come with BMIs above 45, severe bony deformities, active infections, or unusual anatomy that makes standard techniques impossible. These are the cases that most surgeons decline — and the cases Dr. Allahbadia routinely accepts. This page documents five such cases in detail: the challenge, the approach, and the outcome.
