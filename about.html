Surgical Philosophy

The Surgeon Behind the Robot

Dr. Vivek Allahbadia is one of Mumbai's most trusted orthopaedic surgeons, with a practice built over 26 years of dedicated service at Hinduja Healthcare Surgical, Khar. As Head of Department for Orthopaedics, he leads a multidisciplinary team that has redefined what patients can expect from knee surgery.

His philosophy is rooted in a simple belief: every patient deserves a surgery tailored to their exact anatomy — not a one-size-fits-all approach. This conviction led him to become an early adopter and advocate of the Smith+Nephew CORI robotic system at Hinduja Khar, making it one of Mumbai's premier centres for robotic-assisted knee replacement.

After completing his Fellowship in Joint Replacement at the Northern General Hospital, Sheffield, UK, Dr. Allahbadia returned to India with a commitment to bringing world-class orthopaedic care to his patients. He specialises in complex primary and revision knee replacements, arthroscopy, sports injuries, and has pioneered techniques for knee replacement in obese patients, published in the Indian Journal of Orthopaedics.

"A robotic tool is only as good as the surgeon directing it. My 26 years of experience and the CORI system work together to give every patient the outcome they deserve."
