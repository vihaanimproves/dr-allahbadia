Locked Pelvis — A Case Report

Solitary Synovial Chondroma — A Case Report

Case Report

A case study of a solitary synovial chondroma — a rare benign cartilaginous tumour arising from joint synovium — published in one of India's premier postgraduate medical journals.

Plastic Deformation of the Forearm — A Case Report

Case Report

Plastic bowing of the forearm — a distinct and uncommon paediatric injury — documented as a case report, contributing to awareness of a fracture type easily missed on standard radiographs.

Bombay Hospital Journal
Bombay

05

Failed Knee Arthrodesis in Tuberculous Arthritis with Femoral & Tibial Bone Loss and Fixed Equinus Contracture — Treated with the Ilizarov Fixator

Case Report · Complex Reconstruction

A technically demanding case of a failed knee arthrodesis following tuberculous arthritis — compounded by significant bone loss at both the femur and tibia, and a fixed equinus deformity at the ankle — successfully treated using the Ilizarov circular fixator system. A landmark case in Dr. Allahbadia's early career demonstrating his philosophy of limb salvage over amputation.

Journal

Bombay Hospital Journal
Bombay
