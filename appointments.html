From the desk of Dr. Allahbadia

Knee pain is debilitating — but the road to recovery doesn't have to be.

After attaining decades of surgical experience, I want you to know: You don't have to live with this pain. With Robotic Assisted Surgery, recovery is faster and more precise than ever. It doesn't have to be a frightening or painful experience — modern robotic technology has fundamentally changed what patients feel before, during, and after the procedure.

My first priority is always to explore every non-surgical route available. Physiotherapy, injections, lifestyle changes — all are considered carefully before you are laid on the surgical table. When surgery is the right choice, the best surgical route is devised and advised to you personally.

Patients from Across the World

International Patients — You are welcome here.

Dr. Allahbadia accepts patients from the Gulf, East Africa, South-East Asia, the UK, and beyond. Here is exactly how the process works — from your first email to your flight home.

mail
Step 1 · Before You Travel

Send your reports first

Email X-rays, MRIs, blood work, and prior surgical history before booking your flight. Dr. Allahbadia reviews them personally and evaluates your suitability — so you are not making the journey unnecessarily.

health_and_safety
Step 2 · Pre-Travel Prep

Stabilise existing conditions

  • — Diabetes — blood sugar brought to a safe range
  • — Infections — any active infection fully resolved
  • — Blood Pressure — managed and stable before departure
flight_land
Step 3 · On Arrival in Mumbai

Pre-op workup & cardiology clearance

Full pre-operative evaluation at Hinduja Khar — including cardiology clearance. Surgery is formally scheduled only once every clearance is confirmed.

precision_manufacturing
Step 4 · Surgery Day

CORI Robotic Surgery

Faster, more precise, minimal tissue damage — ideal for international patients who cannot afford a prolonged stay. Most patients are walking within 24 hours and discharged after a single overnight stay.

apartment
Step 5 · Post-Surgery Stay

3–4 weeks near the hospital

Khar has well-regarded Airbnbs and serviced apartments close by. Pain-free after discharge, with active physiotherapist care and regular check-ins throughout your stay.

flight_takeoff
Step 6 · Journey Home

Cleared to fly, medications in hand

Discharged with a full course of oral medications — blood thinners, pain management, prescriptions — plus detailed written instructions and an emergency contact number for a safe journey home.
